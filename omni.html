Omnichannel Retail Performance Analysis

Project Overview:
This project focuses on analyzing retail business performance across customers, products, and pricing strategies using Power BI. The goal was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful, actionable insights that support strategic business decision-making.

Dashboard Structure:

  • Overview Dashboard: High-level KPIs and performance tracking.
  • Customer Analysis: Segmentation, behavior, and profitability by loyalty levels.
  • Product Analysis: Profitability, pricing gaps, and category performance.

Key Features:

  • Interactive dashboards with filters and drill-down capabilities.
  • Advanced tooltip pages for deeper insights on hover.
  • Dynamic insight generation based on user interaction.
  • Profit and margin analysis using pricing data (MSRP vs Standard Cost).

Technical Implementation:

  • Data modeling using a star schema approach.
  • DAX measures for KPIs such as Profit, Margin, and AOV.
  • Conditional formatting and visual optimization.
  • Scatter plot analysis for pricing strategy evaluation.

Key Insights:

  • High-value customer segments contribute significantly to overall profit.
  • Certain products generate high revenue but lower profitability.
  • Pricing differences reveal strong margin improvement opportunities.
  • Performance varies across product categories and customer segments.

This project demonstrates the ability to combine advanced data modeling, analytical thinking, and interactive visualization to deliver business-focused intelligence solutions that drive measurable impact.
