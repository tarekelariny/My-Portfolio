Bank Credit Risk Analysis

This project analyzes bank loan portfolio risk using advanced Power BI modeling to evaluate default patterns, customer segmentation, and credit exposure. The objective was to identify high-risk segments and support data-driven credit policy decisions.

Portfolio Overview:

  • Total Customers: 33K
  • Total Loan Amount: \$312M
  • Overall Default Rate: 19%
  • Total Defaulted Amount: \$58M

Risk Segmentation Analysis:

  • Mid-income customers show the highest default rate (30%)
  • Unemployed segment records the highest employment-based default rate (21%)
  • "Other" home ownership category shows elevated risk (30%)
  • Age group 60+ demonstrates increased credit risk (27%)

Technical Implementation:

  • Star schema data modeling for loan & customer datasets
  • DAX measures for Default Rate, Default Amount & Risk Ratios
  • Segment-based drill-down analysis
  • Dynamic filters by country, gender, education & employment type

Business Impact:

  • Identified high-risk segments for improved credit approval criteria
  • Highlighted pricing & portfolio risk exposure areas
  • Provided actionable insights to reduce default losses
