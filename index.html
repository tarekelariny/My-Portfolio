Featured Project

Omnichannel Retail Performance Analysis

Project Overview:
This project focuses on analyzing credit risk within a bank loan portfolio using Power BI. The objective was to assess default patterns across customer demographics, employment stability, income segments, and loan exposure in order to support strategic credit risk management decisions. The analysis transforms raw loan and customer data into structured, risk-focused insights to enhance portfolio monitoring and minimize financial exposure.

Dashboard Structure:

  • Portfolio Overview: High-level KPIs including total customers (33K), total loan exposure (\$312M), default rate (19%), and defaulted amount (\$58M).
  • Demographic Risk Analysis: Default rates by age group, gender, marital status, and education level.
  • Income & Employment Risk: Risk distribution across income groups and employment types.
  • Housing & Ownership Risk: Credit risk patterns based on home ownership categories.
  • Geographical Analysis: Default rate analysis by country and region with drill-down capabilities.

Key Analytical Features:

  • Interactive dashboards with advanced filtering and drill-down functionality.
  • Dynamic KPI tracking for real-time default monitoring.
  • Segment-level risk benchmarking against portfolio averages.
  • Visual risk threshold indicators for identifying high-risk segments.
  • Comparative analysis of defaulted vs non-defaulted loan exposure.

Technical Implementation:

  • Star schema data modeling integrating customer, loan, and demographic datasets.
  • Advanced DAX measures for Default Rate, Default Amount, Risk Contribution, and Portfolio Exposure.
  • Calculated columns for income binning, age grouping, and experience segmentation.
  • Conditional formatting and reference lines to highlight above-average risk segments.
  • Map visualization integration for geographical credit risk monitoring.

Key Risk Insights:

  • Mid-income customers demonstrate the highest default rate (30%), indicating elevated risk concentration within this segment.
  • Unemployed customers exhibit the highest employment-based default rate (21%), emphasizing employment stability as a critical credit factor.
  • The "Other" home ownership category shows the highest default rate (30%), signaling increased risk exposure.
  • Customers aged 60+ present elevated default rates (27%), suggesting age-based risk sensitivity.
  • Overall portfolio default rate stands at 19%, representing \$58M in defaulted exposure out of \$312M total loans.

Business Impact:

  • Identified high-risk borrower segments for improved credit approval criteria.
  • Supported risk-based pricing strategies and loan structuring decisions.
  • Enabled data-driven monitoring of portfolio health and default exposure.
  • Provided actionable insights to reduce potential credit losses and strengthen risk governance.

This project demonstrates strong analytical capabilities in financial risk assessment, data modeling, and business intelligence reporting, combining technical expertise with strategic credit risk evaluation to deliver measurable business value.
